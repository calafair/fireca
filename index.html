About Us

Fireca Technologies is a startup founded in 2019. Our site was launched on May 13, 2021 in the alpha version.
We wanted to provide a system to help unclutter your desktop and browser by creating a online desktop environment.
Our legacy product, Fireca Framework, was discontinued in October 2020. Fireca is privacy focused, so we don't collect your private data.
You can choose to send anonymous data to help us improve Fireca. Learn More

Key features of Fireca Webtop

Responsive

Webtop works smoothly across all devices and operating systems. It is designed to be used on a laptop or computer.

Speed

Fireca runs extremely fast, allowing you to get things done faster than you ever thought you could.

Design

Fireca's simple and elegant design blends seamlessly with your computer, while keeping your workspace uncluttered.

Support

We are constantly updating Fireca Webtop, ensuring it's up to date with the latest web standards. Our support team is always there to help.

We know design.

We created Fireca with style in mind. It's stunning design and eyecatching features are guaranteed to surprise you.
